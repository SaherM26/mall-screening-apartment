Why this property

It has the mix most malls are trying to become.

MOA sits minutes from Minneapolis, St. Paul, and MSP International Airport. That mix of locals, tourists, hotels, transit, and indoor attractions gives partners more than a storefront. It gives them a reason for people to come.

Retail leasing

A useful place for flagships, first stores, pop-ups, and food concepts.

Own the destination moment.

A flagship at MOA can feel like part store, part attraction, and part launch event. The audience is already there, which makes the first impression easier.

Best for: global retail, sport, beauty, tech, lifestyle.

Enter the Midwest with built-in demand.

For brands entering the Midwest, MOA gives visibility quickly. It is easier to test a new concept when the location already has a built-in audience.

Best for: digitally native brands, premium concepts, category leaders.

Test visibly and learn quickly.

Short-term retail can connect to holidays, creator events, product drops, and big traffic weekends without needing a permanent store first.

Best for: fandom, campaigns, holiday demand, product drops.

Turn dining into destination dwell.

Luxury + lifestyle

The opportunity is not just foot traffic. It is the range of the trip: fashion, beauty, wellness, hotel guests, families, tourists, and shoppers who are already spending time in the building.
